About Me



“The best gift anyone can give, I believe, is the gift of sharing themselves.” —Oprah



Welcome! I'm John Ryan Romero, a passionate digital artist and photographer dedicated to capturing the beauty of the world through my lens and imagination. With a background in visual arts and months of experience working with both digital media and traditional photography, I strive to blend creativity with technical expertise in every project I undertake.Photography is my way of telling stories, whether through portraits, landscapes, or abstract compositions. As a digital artist, I sometimes combine these photographs with digital techniques to craft unique and evocative pieces that reflect my imagination. I draw inspiration from other digital art, game characters, and the ever-changing digital world, always seeking new ways to express ideas visually. Whether it's through a camera or a digital canvas, my work aims to provoke thought, stir emotions, and leave a lasting impression. Explore my portfolio to see how I push the boundaries of digital art and photography in the pursuit of creative expression.

Skills & Work Experience




Photography

I've been exploring photography for over months, capturing everything from portraits to landscapes. My journey began with a simple imagination, but quickly evolved to a better camera phone as I honed my skills in composition, lighting, and editing. My experience spans digital photography, post-processing techniques, and creating visual stories that resonate with my self. Whether shooting in natural light or using studio setups, my goal is always to create compelling images that shows and capture the essence of each moment.

Digital Art

As a digital artist, I've spent several weeks refining my craft using tools like Ibis paint and krita. My experience spans digital illustration and photo manipulation, creating artwork for personal projects and Hobbies. I love blending traditional techniques with digital mediums to bring unique ideas to life. Whether it's designing logos, creating digital paintings, or illustrating characters, I'm always experimenting with new styles and concepts. Each project allows me to push creative boundaries and continually evolve as an artist, turning concepts into art.

Editing

As a beginner in editing, I have been learning and experimenting with various editing software such as Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, and Capcut. I'm excited to expand my editing knowledge and refine my techniques, constantly exploring new tools and approaches to improve the overall quality of my work.
